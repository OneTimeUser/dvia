About

Data Visualization and Information Aesthetics (DVIA) is an introductory course on the design and communication of data. The class prioritizes exploration first and foremost, with an emphasis on communication efficacy and aesthetics.

Works represented here are the final culmination of of five week-long visualization design sprints on the fundamentals as well as a midterm challenge based on one of five "real world" scenarios. These projects are entirely student-devised concepts and executions.

This class is program/language agnostic and forces each student to find their own manner of visualization be it hand-drawn, D3.js, tableau, physical, augmented, and beyond.

To learn more about the course and its structure, don't hesitate to get in touch!
