Thy Neighborhood

The word "favela" in Portuguese was originated from the favela-branca plant, a typical tree of the caatingas ecosystem present in the Brazilian state of Bahia, where in the municipality of Monte Santo, exists a hill covered by this plant that was called Morro da Favella and that was base of a battle that took place in Brazil between 1896 and 1897 (Guerra de Canudos). As the guerrilla fighters left Bahia to return to the, at the time, capital of Rio de Janeiro, they settled in an area called Morro da ProvidÃªncia that, because of the presence of the combatants, soon was also known as Morro da Favela.

The importance of that region was such that, starting in 1920, the work favela was now used not only to call the Morro da Favella per se, but also every region in the city that was formed by illegal conglomerations, normally up the hills of the city. Fast forward to nowadays and favela is a work known globally, with popular songs, movies, international attention, tourists tours. With its problems and its glories. But most of all, full of stories. So, know your Thy Neighborhood.
